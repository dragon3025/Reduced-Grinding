=== INFO ===
Open Source at GitHub. Report issues there or on the forum page.

This mod reduces the need for tedious grinding and adds other quality of life features. It's also very configurable:

=== Feature Summary ===
(See the wiki at terrariamods.wiki.gg for more info).
- "WikiThis" mod support.
- Increased item chances for rare enemy loot drops.
- Custom battle potions that will push the spawn rate past vanilla.
- Unlimited quests per day, new Bumblebee Tuna quests are announced, and Honey rewards can be shimmered into each other.
- Bobber potions to increase the amount of fishing bobbers used.
- Merchant Dice to re-roll the Traveling Merchant shop. Available after the 3 Mech bosses. Re-rolls before Moonlord: 3, re-rolls after: 10.
- Merchant Dice to re-roll the Traveling Merchant shop. Its availability and uses per day depends on progression.
- Items to stop or extend invasions. Post-game items to summon or remove Lunar Pillars.
- Chaos Elementals will replace most spawns for 6 minutes after defeating Queen Slime.
- Universal Pylon can be crafted out of all other Pylons. You can also set it to require Boss Souls or Fragments. To keep Potion of Return useful, the Pylon requires 2+ villagers while the Bestiary is incomplete; this can be disabled.
- Custom generation to place chest loot, when the vanilla chance fails.
- Shimmer Feathers or Clouds to control weather. Use Moon Watch to control the Moon phase.
- Before reaching the Shimmer, Faelings will help you find them.
- Item values are adjusted to match their new rate.
- Plus more.
